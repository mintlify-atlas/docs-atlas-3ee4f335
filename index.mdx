Mi App Números is an interactive, child-friendly web application built with React and Vite that makes learning numbers 1–11 fun and effective. It combines three complementary learning modes — step-by-step exploration, multiple-choice quiz, and freehand canvas tracing — all wrapped in colourful animations and a star-reward system to keep young learners engaged.

## How it works

Mi App Números presents numbers through three learning modes that progressively reinforce recognition, counting, and writing skills.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Launch the app in your browser. The main menu offers two session types: **Paso a Paso** (sequential, 1–11) and **Modo Aleatorio** (random order for advanced practice).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn a number">
    The Learn view shows the numeral, its Spanish word, and an animated grid of emojis — one emoji per unit — so children connect quantity to symbol visually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice counting">
    Tap **Practicar** to enter Practice Mode. A grid of emojis appears and three number buttons are shown. Choose the correct count to earn a star.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trace the number">
    Tap **Escribir** to open the canvas. A ghost numeral guides the stroke. Draw with a mouse or finger, then tap **¡Listo!** to confirm and earn a star.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The star counter in the top navigation bar persists throughout the session, so children can track their progress as they move through all eleven numbers.
</Tip>
